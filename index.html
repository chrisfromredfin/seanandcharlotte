A Wedding During Covid-19

Dearest friends and family,

Thank you so much for celebrating with us on our wedding day. It was the most wonderful day of our lives and it would not have been the same without you. We are truly grateful for your generosity and presence on our special day.

The pandemic surely complicated planning and setup for the wedding, and we sincerely thank everyone for following precautions and guidelines, and helping to keep us safe during Covid-19. We are very happy and grateful for your cooperation during our special day. So far, no one has any symptoms of Covid-19. For those who were not able to attend our special day, the video and photographs are available.

- Sean & Charlotte
